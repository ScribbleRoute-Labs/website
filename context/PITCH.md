
The design philosophy is local first. 

A toybox built for toddlers. 
1) Enough games to not get bored.
2) Games that grow with your child.
3) Agency without escape.
4) Games that teach accidentally.
5) Fun without addiction.

Some of the things your kid can learn:
1) Color matching.
2) Eye hand cordination tapping bugs
3) Drawing letters and writing their name.
4) How colors mix together to make new colors.
5) Animals and examples of things they eat.

Offline and Privacy First
1) Parent admin app is available for local use.
2) No outbound requests until you ask for it.
3) Optional Google Account for true remote access.

Volume Limits
1) All audio is optional
2) Volume max locked to parent control

Parental Control:
1) Disable any game at any time.
2) Tweak games to scale difficulty and meet your child where they are.
3) Set time limits. Gentle shuts down when time is up.
4) Save drawings and signatures to view your child's art after the fact.
